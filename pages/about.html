Vision

Our aim is to increase the added value provided by the product. For example, we aim to make our product environment friendly by reducing the consumption of electricity, we also aim to help ease the life of housewives and increase her quality time through providing more spare time for them. Mainly, we aim to find our product in over 60% of houses in Egypt within the next 7 years. Through ingenuity and dedication, this newcomer's vision was to change the entire world of washing and ironing. There was even more. Washing used to be a difficult task until the invention of washing machines. People were doing their laundry and iron by hand and spending hours per day on household chores. Being a leading producer and trader of household cleaning goods in Egypt , with secure growth strategies, sound distribution networks, and technological advancement leadership to provide the highest added value to shareholders, workers, and society.
